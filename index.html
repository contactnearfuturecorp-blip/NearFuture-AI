How many languages does it support? +

NearFuture AI currently supports over 50 languages including English, Spanish, French, German, Arabic, Filipino, Japanese, Korean, and many more. We're constantly adding new languages based on customer needs.

Can it really handle complex order questions? +

Yes! Our AI is trained to handle complex order inquiries including product recommendations, inventory checks, shipping estimates, and even processing returns. For extremely complex issues, it can seamlessly escalate to a human agent.
